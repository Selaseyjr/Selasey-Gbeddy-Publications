# Supply Chain Research Series

Welcome to my collection of independent research articles exploring logistics, supply chain management, global trade, sustainability, operational intelligence, and emerging economies.

This repository documents my journey as an aspiring logistics researcher and supply chain professional. Through these publications, I aim to explore how logistics systems have evolved throughout history, how they shape modern civilization, and how innovation, analytics, and sustainability can contribute to their future development.
